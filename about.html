About me

Designing with
curiosity & purpose.

Hi, I’m Anya — a graphic designer creating digital products and visual concepts.

I have been passionate about design for over five years and have more than three years of professional experience. I began with graphic design, earned a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, and completed additional courses to expand my knowledge across different design disciplines.

Today, I focus on digital products and visual concepts. I care about clear structure, strong composition, thoughtful details, and design that feels intuitive rather than overexplained.

I also integrate AI tools into my workflow to explore ideas faster, improve efficiency, and open up new creative directions while keeping the final decisions intentional and human.

New tools help me see familiar problems from a different angle.

Lately, I have been exploring Figma Make, Spline, Claude, and Nano Banana. I use them to learn, prototype, experiment, and bring early ideas to life in new ways.

I believe that staying curious and constantly trying new things is one of the best ways to grow as a designer. Tools may change, but curiosity, judgment, and a clear point of view remain essential.
